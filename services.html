The Provigood Method

A clear, transparent process, from briefing to delivery. We work for you, or on behalf of you, depending on what your project needs.

  1. 1

    We co-define the scope

    We listen. Together, we define the contents of each assignment, the desired outcomes, and the success metrics. No vague missions, no surprises.

  2. 2

    From idea to action

    We turn your idea into a strategy, your strategy into a plan, and your plan into action. Each project gets a clear roadmap with milestones, owners, and deliverables.

  3. 3

    Detailed proposal

    Once your objectives, mission and budget are clear, we provide a detailed offer of assistance with deliverables, timeline, fees, and assumptions. You commit only after full alignment.

    Detailed time-tracking sheets, expense claims, and milestone reports throughout the engagement. You always know where your project, and your investment, stand.

Frequently asked questions

Can I engage Provigood on just one service, or do I have to take the full 360°? +

You can engage us on a single service (e.g. a market entry study, an executive search mission, or distribution of a specific product line) or on multiple services in parallel. The 360° label reflects our capability, not an obligation. Most engagements start with one focused project, and expand if and when the partnership delivers value.

Which sectors and industries does Provigood serve? +

Our core expertise is food, beverage, FMCG/CPG, retail and hospitality. We also support adjacent sectors when projects involve cold chain, sourcing, distribution or talent, including agriculture, agribusiness and pharmaceuticals. We do not work in sectors where we cannot bring deep operational expertise.

How are your services priced? +

We offer three pricing models depending on the engagement: fixed-fee for scoped projects (most common for market entry, recruitment, training), monthly retainer for ongoing operational support, and success-based fees for distribution partnerships and certain commercial missions. Every proposal is detailed and transparent, including time tracking and expense claims throughout the engagement.
