How we work

Executive search process

  1. 1

    Brief and profile definition

    We work with you to define the role, the must-have skills, the cultural fit, the compensation range, and the success metrics. We share salary benchmarks and market intelligence to anchor expectations.

  2. 2

    Sourcing and long list

    We activate Provigood's, Synovivo's and Manageria's networks plus targeted research to build a long list of 15 to 25 qualified candidates within 2 to 4 weeks.

  3. 3

    Assessment and short list

    We screen, interview and assess each candidate against your brief. You receive a short list of 3 to 5 qualified candidates with detailed profiles, motivation summaries, and salary expectations.

  4. 4

    Interview support and decision

    We coordinate interviews, provide debriefs after each round, and support negotiation, reference checks and offer crafting. We are present at every step.

  5. 5

    Onboarding and 90-day follow-up

    We follow up with both you and the new hire at 30, 60 and 90 days to ensure smooth integration and rapid impact. Our success is your hire's success.

Training process

  1. 1

    Needs assessment

    We diagnose with you and your team where the real capability gaps are. We don't sell off-the-shelf programs, we sell custom solutions to specific business problems.

  2. 2

    Custom program design

    We co-design the program: content, duration, format, language, group size, case studies. You validate before we go to delivery.

  3. 3

    Delivery

    Our facilitators run the sessions: in-person, hybrid, English, French or Vietnamese. Engagement is high because content is anchored in your real business.

  4. 4

    Follow-up and impact measurement

    We measure impact 30 to 60 days after delivery: behavior change, business outcomes, team feedback. Refinements happen through one-on-one coaching when relevant.

Frequently asked questions — Provigoodpeople

What kind of executive search do you handle? +

Provigoodpeople handles executive search across the full talent spectrum, from C-level executives (CEO, COO, CFO, Country Manager) and senior experts to A and B level managers. Our focus is food, FMCG, agribusiness, hospitality and retail. We work in close partnership with Synovivo (specialized in agricultural talents) and Manageria (specialized in agro-food talents), giving us unparalleled access to industry-specific candidates, both Vietnamese and international.

What training programs are available? +

Our training catalog covers leadership and management, sales and negotiation, communication, AI productivity, agile project management, cultural intelligence, and customized workshops aligned with your strategic priorities. Programs are delivered in-person, hybrid, or remote, in English, French or Vietnamese, and adapted to your team's specific business reality. Our most requested program is structured sales force capability building for FMCG and modern trade teams.

What are typical salary benchmarks in F&B Vietnam? +

Salaries in Vietnam's F&B sector vary significantly by role, location, company type and experience. As a general guide, an experienced country manager in Ho Chi Minh City typically earns USD 60,000 to 120,000 annually, while sales managers in FMCG range from USD 25,000 to 50,000. Hanoi tends to be slightly lower than HCMC for equivalent roles. Provigoodpeople provides custom salary benchmarks as part of every recruitment engagement, with detailed comparisons by sector, function and seniority.

How long does a typical executive search take? +

A typical executive search runs 8 to 12 weeks from kick-off to signed offer, broken down as follows: brief and sourcing (2 to 4 weeks), assessment and short list (2 to 3 weeks), interviews and decision (2 to 3 weeks), and offer negotiation (1 to 2 weeks). Onboarding follow-up extends to 90 days post-hire.

How are recruitment and training services priced? +

Executive search is priced as a percentage of the placed candidate's annual gross compensation (typically 20 to 30 % depending on seniority and complexity), with a structured retainer schedule. Training is priced per program based on duration, group size, customization level and travel. Every proposal includes detailed deliverables, timeline, fees and assumptions.
