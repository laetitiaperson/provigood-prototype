1. Who we are

Provigood ("we", "our", "us") is a company registered in Vietnam under the name CÔNG TY TNHH MỘT THÀNH VIÊN VÀNG TRĂNG TRẺ, headquartered at 16/1, Street 46, An Khanh Ward, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. We operate the website provigood.com (the "Website") and provide consulting, training, executive search, and distribution services in the food, beverage, FMCG, and hospitality sectors.

We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you visit our Website, contact us, subscribe to our newsletter, or engage with our services.

Data Controller: Provigood, represented by Vincent Treluyer, Founder & CEO.
Contact for data protection matters: contact@provigood.com

2. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Visitors to provigood.com and its subdomains
  • Individuals who fill in our contact forms, partnership applications, or newsletter signup
  • Individuals who book calls through our Calendly integration
  • Clients, partners, and prospects whose data we receive through business interactions or referrals

3. Personal data we collect

3.1 Information you provide directly

  • Identification: first name, last name, professional title
  • Contact details: email address, phone number, postal address, country of residence
  • Professional information: company name, industry, function, area of interest
  • Project information: project description, scope, timeline, budget (when shared with us)
  • Communications: messages sent through forms, emails, chat, or messaging platforms
  • Partnership applications: company profile, CV, professional references, experience

3.2 Information collected automatically

  • Technical data: IP address, browser type and version, device type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent, referring website, clicks, language preference
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see our Cookie Policy for details)

3.3 Information from third parties

  • LinkedIn or other professional networks, when you initiate contact with us through these platforms
  • Referrals from existing clients or partners (with their consent)
  • Public business directories, when conducting business development activities

4. Purposes and legal bases for processing

We process your personal data for the purposes set out below. The applicable legal basis (under EU GDPR Article 6) is indicated for each purpose. Equivalent legal bases apply under Vietnamese law (Personal Data Protection Decree) and other applicable jurisdictions.

5. How we share your data

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with the categories of recipients listed below, and only to the extent necessary for the purposes described in Section 4.

5.1 Trusted service providers (data processors)

These providers act under our instructions and are bound by data processing agreements that comply with GDPR and equivalent regulations.

  • Hosting and infrastructure: Cloudflare Inc. (USA, GDPR-compliant via Standard Contractual Clauses)
  • Domain and email services: Gandi SAS (France, EU-based, GDPR-compliant)
  • Appointment scheduling: Calendly LLC (USA, GDPR-compliant via Standard Contractual Clauses)
  • Multilingual website plugin: TranslatePress
  • Newsletter management: [to be defined, e.g., Mailchimp, Brevo, or similar]
  • Analytics: [to be defined, e.g., Plausible, Google Analytics, or similar]

5.2 Business partners

When relevant to a specific project, and only with your explicit consent, we may share your information with:

  • Members of our 250+ expert partner network (legal, marketing, logistics, sourcing, HR, training, finance, corporate services)
  • Synovivo and Manageria, our exclusive Vietnam representation partners, for HR and executive search projects
  • Olivo Cold Logistics, Em Oi Café, and Le Caiffa, for product distribution inquiries

5.3 Legal authorities and third parties

We may disclose your data when required by law, by court order, or by Vietnamese, EU, or other applicable authorities. We may also disclose your data to defend our rights or those of others.

6. International data transfers

Provigood is based in Vietnam. Depending on the service used, your data may be transferred to and processed in:

  • Vietnam (our country of operation and primary place of business)
  • France and the European Economic Area (Gandi servers, GDPR jurisdiction)
  • United States (Cloudflare, Calendly, certain other tools, under Standard Contractual Clauses and other GDPR-recognized safeguards)
  • Other countries where our service providers operate (always with appropriate safeguards)

For all international transfers outside the EEA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses adopted by the European Commission, on Adequacy Decisions where applicable, or on equivalent safeguards under GDPR Article 46.

7. Data retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above:

  • Contact form inquiries (unsuccessful): up to 3 years after last interaction
  • Newsletter subscribers: until you unsubscribe, plus 1 year for legal record-keeping
  • Active clients and partners: duration of the engagement plus 5 years (for legal, accounting, and tax purposes)
  • Partnership applications (not retained): 2 years after submission
  • Technical and analytics data: as defined in our Cookie Policy (typically 13 to 26 months)
  • Legal disputes: until full resolution plus the applicable statute of limitations

8. Your rights

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (if you are in the EU/EEA) and the Vietnamese Personal Data Protection Decree, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — obtain a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to restriction of processing — temporarily limit the use of your data.
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interest or for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, where processing is based on your consent.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — with a supervisory authority (CNIL in France, your national data protection authority in the EU, the Ministry of Public Security in Vietnam, or equivalent in your jurisdiction).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@provigood.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all Website traffic
  • Cloudflare's enterprise-grade security infrastructure (DDoS protection, WAF, bot management)
  • Restricted internal access (only those who need the data for their work)
  • Regular security reviews and updates
  • Non-disclosure agreements with all our partners and service providers
  • Secure password practices and multi-factor authentication where applicable

Despite our best efforts, no transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we treat every reasonable measure as a baseline.

10. Children's privacy

Our Website and services are intended for business users and are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, our service offerings, technology, legal requirements, or other relevant factors. Material changes will be communicated via:

  • A banner on the Website for 30 days
  • An email to active newsletter subscribers
  • Update of the "Effective date" at the top of this page

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the Website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
