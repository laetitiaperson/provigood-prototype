About Provigood

Empowering businesses to succeed in Vietnam since 2018

Provigood is a Ho Chi Minh City–based consulting and trading company, helping food, beverage, FMCG and hospitality companies grow in, from and to Vietnam. Backed by 25+ years of leadership across Asia and a network of 250+ trusted expert partners.

"Excellence with speed and agility, that is our motto and our promise."

From a conviction to a network

2009

The decision

After fifteen years in major international FMCG and retail groups (Casino, P&G, Bel, Lactalis), Vincent Treluyer made a decision: relocate to Vietnam, with his family, to immerse them in the dynamism of Asia and build something of his own.

2018

Provigood is born

After nearly a decade operating in senior roles within Asian FMCG groups, including Dai Tan Viet (now New Viet Dairy), where he saw firsthand the opportunities and obstacles facing international companies in Vietnam, Vincent founded Provigood. The mission was clear: combine the rigor of multinationals with the agility, network and local intelligence of a Saigon-based partner. To deliver excellence, with speed and agility.

Today

A 250+ partner ecosystem

Since 2018, Provigood has supported international corporations, SMEs, and family-owned businesses with their projects in, from and to Vietnam. Three pillars of activity have emerged: strategic consulting and business development, Provigoodpeople (executive search and corporate training), and distribution of innovative, sustainable products. Today, Provigood is at the center of a network of 250+ trusted institutional and private partners across legal, marketing, logistics, sourcing, HR, finance and corporate services.

Our mission and values

We exist to help companies in food, beverage, FMCG and hospitality unlock the full potential of the Vietnamese market, quickly, soundly, and sustainably. Whether you're entering Vietnam for the first time or scaling an existing operation, we provide the strategic vision, operational expertise and trusted network to turn ambition into measurable results.

Integrity

Transparency, honesty and ethical practices in all interactions. With clients, with partners, with the regulators. No grey areas. No hidden fees. No promises we can't keep.

Excellence

High standards and demonstrated expertise to achieve outstanding results. Every deliverable, every introduction, every recommendation reflects 25+ years of operational rigor.

Simplicity

Focus on actions that create real value. We resist the temptation to over-engineer. We strip projects down to what matters and execute with clarity.

A word from the founder

The career

Vincent Treluyer is the founder and CEO of Provigood. Originally from Brittany, France, he holds a 25-year career in strategy, business development, and food, beverage and FMCG industries, including more than 20 years in Asia (Taiwan, China, Vietnam). His leadership track record includes senior positions at Casino Group (5 years), Procter & Gamble (7 years), Bel Group (2 years), Dai Tan Viet (3 years), and Lactalis (5 years).

The journey to Vietnam

Vincent's journey took him from Auvergne and Lyon to Ho Chi Minh City in 2009, driven by a desire to establish a business and immerse his family in Vietnamese culture. In 2018, he founded Provigood, a consulting and trading company assisting firms in food, beverage and consumer goods sectors with their ventures in Vietnam.

Beyond business

Beyond his professional life, Vincent is the drummer of the rock band Mekong Monkey Soup. His passion for gastronomy is evident in his role as Deputy Secretary of the Disciples of Escoffier in Vietnam, an organization dedicated to preserving and passing on culinary expertise. Additionally, Vincent founded La Dalat de Saigon club, celebrating classic cars and the joy of the journey.

"I came to Vietnam in 2009 because I wanted to build something for my clients, for my family, for myself. Eighteen years later, this country still surprises me every week. That's why I love what we do at Provigood."

Recognized by the right institutions

Provigood is proud to be accredited and recognized by leading institutions supporting international business expansion in Asia. These accreditations reflect both our methodological rigor and our commitment to long-term partnerships.

Bretagne Commerce International

Approved service provider

Provigood is an officially approved service provider of Bretagne Commerce International, the regional trade and investment agency of Brittany, France. This accreditation gives Brittany-based companies a trusted bridge to the Vietnamese market, and gives Vietnamese companies access to Provigood's French regional network.

Stratexio

Accredited member

Stratexio is the strategic export accelerator program for French SMEs and mid-cap companies. Provigood's accreditation by Stratexio formalizes our role as a senior partner in supporting French companies in their Vietnam expansion.

Thu Duc Business Association

First foreign member

Provigood is the first foreign member of the Thu Duc Business Association, the business association covering Vietnam's most dynamic industrial district (formerly the Thu Duc District, now part of Ho Chi Minh City's Eastern industrial corridor). This unique position gives our clients direct access to local industrial decision-makers and ecosystems.
