Compostable coffee capsules, made in Vietnam

Em Oi Café and Le Caiffa offer premium, compostable coffee capsules crafted in Vietnam, and high-quality coffee machines designed for capsule brewing.

First established in Ho Chi Minh City in 2009, Bac Tam Tam Ltd. (now CapsuleCup Ltd. in Siem Reap) is the first company in Vietnam to design and produce coffee capsules compatible with most coffee capsule machines. It transforms high-quality and strictly selected Vietnamese coffees, teas, and herbals into sustainable compostable capsules. Arabica and Robusta beans come mainly from Dalat, Son La, and Dak. They are masterfully ground, roasted, and packed in Ho Chi Minh City.

Em Oi Café and Le Caiffa coffee capsules are proudly sold to five-star hotels, retailers, and wholesalers in Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, and Thailand.

Frequently asked questions, Em Oi Café & Le Caiffa

Are Em Oi Café and Le Caiffa capsules truly compostable? +

Yes. Em Oi Café and Le Caiffa capsules are fully compostable and made in Vietnam. They are produced by CapsuleCup Ltd. (formerly Bac Tam Tam Ltd.), the first company in Vietnam to design coffee capsules compatible with most capsule machines. The Arabica and Robusta beans are sourced primarily from Dalat, Son La and Dak provinces, then masterfully ground, roasted and packed in Ho Chi Minh City. The capsules biodegrade naturally and are an eco-friendly alternative to aluminum or plastic capsules.

Where are Em Oi Café and Le Caiffa sold? +

Em Oi Café and Le Caiffa capsules are distributed in 5-star hotels, premium retailers, wholesalers and select restaurants across Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos and Thailand, and are also available through their dedicated Facebook pages. For pricing, samples or distribution inquiries, contact our team via the contact form or by email at contact@provigood.com.

Can we white-label or co-brand Em Oi Café capsules? +

Yes. We offer co-branded and private-label opportunities for B2B partners, subject to volume. We can also supply compatible capsule machines for hospitality and corporate environments and provide sustainability storytelling support: content, certifications and packaging copy. Contact us to discuss volumes and terms.

Can I become a sub-distributor of Em Oi Café in my country? +

Yes, Provigood is open to sub-distribution partnerships across Southeast Asia. We have existing distribution arrangements in Cambodia, Laos and Thailand, and we are actively exploring partnerships in other ASEAN markets. Reach out via our partner page if you have an established food, hospitality or retail network in your country.
