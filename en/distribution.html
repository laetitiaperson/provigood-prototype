Olivo Cold Logistics

Eco-friendly insulated containers for the safe last-mile delivery of temperature-controlled goods

Established in 1956, Olivo is France's leading manufacturer of insulated containers. With more than 65 years of expertise serving food, pharmaceutical and logistics industries worldwide, Olivo brings to Vietnam a proven, sustainable alternative to traditional cold chain solutions. Provigood is the official Olivo Cold Logistics partner in Vietnam.

How Olivo passive cold solutions work

Olivo containers maintain temperature-controlled goods for up to 12 hours (BOX format) or up to 72 hours (BAC and ROLL formats) without mechanical refrigeration or refrigerated trucks. They use eutectic plates or cryogenic cooling sources, allowing reliable cold chain delivery with no electricity, no fuel-burning compressors, and no compromise on safety.

Three formats for every use case

BOX

Light, stackable, ideal for last-mile delivery on motorbikes and cars. Up to 12 hours of cold preservation, reusable 2 000+ times.

BAC

Modular trolleys for medium-distance distribution and merchandising. Up to 72 hours.

ROLL

Large-volume containers for high-volume distribution and warehousing. Up to 72 hours.

Certifications and credentials

  • BAC and ROLL formats certified by CEMAFROID under the A.T.P. (Agreement for Transport of Perishables) standard
  • Lifespan of 12+ years per container
  • BOX format reusable 2 000+ times, fully recyclable

Key benefits for your business

  • Multi-temperature delivery in a single dry truck or motorbike, drastically reducing delivery trips and fuel costs
  • ATP-grade safety and compliance for food and pharmaceutical transportation
  • Sustainable alternative to single-use polystyrene (which pollutes Vietnam's beaches, rivers and soils)
  • Lower CO₂ emissions and reduced urban traffic congestion
  • CSR/ESG-compliant, supports your sustainability reporting

Who is Olivo for?

  • Food and beverage manufacturers
  • Frozen, chilled and ice cream distributors
  • Pharmaceutical companies and clinical trial logistics
  • E-commerce food platforms (Shopee Food, GrabFood, etc.)
  • Quick-service restaurants and centralized kitchens
  • Catering and hospitality groups

Em Oi Café & Le Caiffa

Compostable coffee capsules made in Vietnam, paired with capsule machines for hospitality and retail

Em Oi Café and Le Caiffa are premium, compostable coffee capsules crafted in Vietnam. Originally launched in Ho Chi Minh City in 2009, the brand was the first in Vietnam to design and produce capsules compatible with most capsule machines on the market. Today, the production has expanded across Southeast Asia, with carefully selected Arabica and Robusta beans sourced primarily from Dalat, Son La and Dak provinces, masterfully roasted and packed in Ho Chi Minh City.

Why compostable coffee capsules matter

Standard coffee capsules generate massive plastic and aluminum waste, billions of capsules each year end up in landfills or oceans. Em Oi Café and Le Caiffa capsules are fully compostable, biodegrading naturally without harming Vietnamese soils, rivers or ecosystems. They deliver the same convenience and quality as traditional capsules, with a fraction of the environmental footprint.

  • Wholesalers in Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos and Thailand
  • Select restaurants and specialty cafés

Frequently asked questions, Distribution

How does Olivo Cold Logistics work? +

Olivo Cold Logistics, a French manufacturer founded in 1956 and represented by Provigood in Vietnam, designs passive insulated containers that maintain temperature-controlled goods without mechanical refrigeration. Using eutectic plates or cryogenic sources, Olivo containers preserve cold chain integrity for up to 12 hours (BOX format) or 72 hours (BAC and ROLL formats). They are A.T.P.-certified for transport of perishables, last 12+ years in operation, and allow multi-temperature deliveries in a single dry truck or motorbike, drastically reducing fuel consumption, CO₂ emissions and last-mile costs.

Are Em Oi Café and Le Caiffa capsules truly compostable? +

Yes. Em Oi Café and Le Caiffa capsules are fully compostable and made in Vietnam. They are produced by CapsuleCup Ltd. (formerly Bac Tam Tam Ltd.), the first company in Vietnam to design coffee capsules compatible with most capsule machines. The Arabica and Robusta beans are sourced primarily from Dalat, Son La and Dak provinces, then masterfully ground, roasted and packed in Ho Chi Minh City. The capsules biodegrade naturally and are an eco-friendly alternative to aluminum or plastic capsules.

Where can I find or buy your products? +

Em Oi Café and Le Caiffa capsules are distributed in 5-star hotels, premium retailers, wholesalers and select restaurants across Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos and Thailand, and are also available through their dedicated Facebook pages. Olivo Cold Logistics insulated containers are available B2B for food manufacturers, distributors, retailers and logistics operators handling temperature-sensitive products. For pricing, technical specifications, sample requests or distribution inquiries on either product line, contact our team via the contact form or by email at contact@provigood.com.

Can I become a sub-distributor of Olivo or Em Oi Café in my country? +

Yes, Provigood is open to sub-distribution partnerships across Southeast Asia. We have existing distribution arrangements in Cambodia, Laos and Thailand, and we are actively exploring partnerships in other ASEAN markets. Reach out via our partner page if you have an established food, hospitality or logistics network in your country.

What sizes and customizations are available for Olivo containers? +

Olivo offers three core formats, BOX, BAC and ROLL, in multiple sizes adapted to use cases ranging from motorbike last-mile delivery to high-volume warehousing. Custom branding, color and layout options are available for B2B partners depending on volume. Contact us for a detailed catalog and quote.
