Why Vietnam, Why now?

Vietnam is one of the world's fastest-growing markets for food, FMCG, retail and hospitality. With a young, urbanizing population of over 100 million, rising purchasing power, and an explosive appetite for premium and international brands, Vietnam offers a unique window of opportunity for ambitious companies.

It is also a privileged trade hub thanks to landmark agreements such as EVFTA, CPTPP and RCEP, opening Vietnam to European, North American and Asian exporters under exceptional conditions. And it serves as a strategic gateway to the wider ASEAN region.

But success in Vietnam takes more than ambition. It demands local expertise, reliable partners, and an agile strategy. That's exactly what we deliver.

Frequently asked questions

What does Provigood do? +

Provigood is a Ho Chi Minh City–based consulting and trading company, helping food, beverage, FMCG and hospitality companies grow in, from and to Vietnam. We deliver three integrated activities: strategic consulting, executive search and training (Provigoodpeople), and distribution of select products.

How do I enter the Vietnamese food market? +

Our 4-step approach: understand the market, accelerate growth, optimize operations, and scale sustainably. We handle research, legal setup, partner identification, branding and operations, tailored to your sector and timeline.

How do I become a Provigood partner? +

We welcome experts and firms in legal, marketing, logistics, sourcing, HR, training, finance and corporate services. Apply via our partner page, we'll set up an initial discovery call and explore mutual fit.
