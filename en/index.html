The challenges of expanding into Vietnam

Expanding into Vietnam can feel overwhelming: regulations, distribution channels, supplier management, talent recruitment, and brand localization all demand time and expertise. Without the right guidance, businesses risk wasting resources and missing opportunities.

Why choose Provigood?

At Provigood, we've been helping businesses successfully enter and thrive in Vietnam since 2018. Led by founder Vincent Treluyer, with over 25 years of business expertise in Asia, we know what it takes to turn challenges into profitable growth.

Why Vietnam, why now?

Vietnam is one of the world's fastest-growing markets for FMCG, retail, and consumer products. But market success demands more than just ambition. You need local expertise, reliable partners, and an agile strategy. That's exactly what we deliver.

What does Provigood do? +

Provigood is a Ho Chi Minh City–based consulting and trading company, helping food, beverage, FMCG and hospitality companies grow in, from and to Vietnam. We deliver integrated services across market entry, business development, team empowerment, and corporate services.

How do I enter the Vietnamese food market? +

Our 4-step approach: understand the market, accelerate growth, optimize operations, and scale sustainably. We handle research, legal setup, partner identification, branding and operations, tailored to your sector and timeline.

How do I become a Provigood partner? +

We welcome experts and firms in legal, marketing, logistics, sourcing, HR, training, finance and corporate services. Apply via our partner page, we'll set up an initial discovery call and explore mutual fit.
