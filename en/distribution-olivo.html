Eco-friendly insulated containers for safe last-mile delivery

Provigood is the official Olivo Cold Logistics partner in Vietnam, distributing passive cold-chain solutions for temperature-controlled goods across Vietnam and Southeast Asia.

Discover Olivo Cold Logistics passive cold solutions

Olivo Cold Logistics insulated containers enable the safe transportation of temperature-controlled goods for up to 12 hours (BOX) and 72 hours (BAC, ROLL) without mechanical refrigeration or cold trucks. These containers use eutectic plates or cryogenic sources for cooling.

Key benefits

  • Transport multiple temperature-sensitive goods in the same dry truck or motorbike, reducing delivery trips and costs.
  • Ensure the highest level of safety and compliance for food and pharmaceutical transportation.
  • BAC and ROLL containers are certified by CEMAFROID under the A.T.P. (Agreement for Transport of Perishables) standard and can be used for up to 12 years.
  • The BOX container is reusable (over 2,000 times), recyclable, and a sustainable alternative to single-use polystyrene foam boxes, which pollute Vietnam's beaches, rivers, and soils.

Sustainability & ESG impact

Olivo Cold Logistics insulated containers help businesses and contractors achieve CSR and ESG compliance by:

  1. Securing the cold chain during picking, loading, and unloading, increasing food safety and reducing waste.
  2. Reducing delivery trips by combining various temperature-controlled goods in one vehicle, cutting fuel consumption and CO2 emissions while improving urban traffic flow.
  3. Enabling dry truck deliveries via cars, bikes, or trucks, lowering energy consumption and delivery costs.
  4. Replacing single-use polystyrene foam boxes with reusable, durable, and recyclable alternatives, reducing non-recyclable plastic waste.

Frequently asked questions, Olivo Cold Logistics

What is Olivo Cold Logistics and how does it work? +

Olivo Cold Logistics, a French manufacturer founded in 1956 and represented by Provigood in Vietnam, designs passive insulated containers that maintain temperature-controlled goods without mechanical refrigeration. Using eutectic plates or cryogenic sources, Olivo containers preserve cold chain integrity for up to 12 hours (BOX format) or 72 hours (BAC and ROLL formats). They are A.T.P.-certified for transport of perishables, last 12+ years in operation, and allow multi-temperature deliveries in a single dry truck or motorbike, drastically reducing fuel consumption, CO₂ emissions and last-mile costs.

What formats does Olivo offer? +

Olivo offers three core formats, BOX, BAC and ROLL, in multiple sizes adapted to use cases ranging from motorbike last-mile delivery to high-volume warehousing. Custom branding, color and layout options are available for B2B partners depending on volume. Contact us for a detailed catalog and quote.

Where is Olivo used? +

Olivo Cold Logistics insulated containers are available B2B for food manufacturers, distributors, retailers and logistics operators handling temperature-sensitive products across Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos and Thailand. Typical users include frozen and chilled distributors, pharmaceutical companies, e-commerce food platforms, quick-service restaurants and catering and hospitality groups. For pricing, technical specifications, sample requests or distribution inquiries, contact our team via the contact form or by email at contact@provigood.com.

Yes, Provigood is open to sub-distribution partnerships across Southeast Asia. We have existing distribution arrangements in Cambodia, Laos and Thailand, and we are actively exploring partnerships in other ASEAN markets. Reach out via our partner page if you have an established food, hospitality or logistics network in your country.
