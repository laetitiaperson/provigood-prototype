Why partner with Provigood

Joining Provigood's partner network is more than landing one project. It is plugging into a curated, established ecosystem that has been delivering results for international and local food, FMCG and hospitality clients for 8+ years. Five concrete benefits.

Access to high-quality projects

We work with international corporations (Panzani, Lactalis, Andros, Carambar & Co), growing SMEs and family-owned businesses entering or scaling in Vietnam. Our projects are well-scoped, well-funded, and well-managed, meaning you focus on delivery, not on chasing payments or fixing scope creep.

A network that opens doors

Our 250+ partners include leading institutional players (Bretagne Commerce International, Stratexio, EuroCham, CCI France Vietnam, TBA), specialized firms (Fidal Asiattorneys, Cimigo, FM Logistic, Sopexa, Synovivo, Manageria) and trusted independent experts. Joining Provigood means joining this ecosystem, and gaining warm introductions you would not get on your own.

Visibility through Partner Spotlights

Active partners are featured in our Partner Spotlight series on the blog and in client briefings. We promote your expertise to our clients, on LinkedIn, and at events. Your work gets seen, and credited.

Aligned, sector-focused engagements

We only work in food, beverage, FMCG and hospitality. That means every project you receive from Provigood is aligned with sectoral expertise. No tourism gigs disguised as F&B. No financial services briefs disguised as FMCG. Pure, focused, value-creating engagements.

Fair, transparent commercial terms

We share project economics openly. We negotiate fees in good faith. We pay on time. The best partner relationships in Vietnam last 10+ years, and that requires trust on both sides, day in and day out.

Five partnership models we use

Different partners contribute differently. We adapt the model to the type of value created, never the other way around.

  1. 1

    Strategic alliances

    Long-term collaboration to co-develop market entry strategies and complex projects. Resources, expertise and risk are shared. Used for major client engagements requiring multiple specialized firms.

  2. 2

    Joint ventures

    Formal joint venture structures to access new markets, share risks and capitalize on combined strengths. Used for distribution partnerships, co-investments and ASEAN expansion vehicles.

  3. 3

    Distribution partnerships

    Commission or margin-based partnerships with local distributors for specific product lines (Olivo Cold Logistics, Em Oi Café / Le Caiffa). Geography-specific exclusivity is possible based on volume commitments.

  4. 4

    Technology partnerships

    Integration of innovative technology solutions to enhance operational efficiency and customer engagement. Used with software vendors, platforms, e-commerce specialists and digital marketing agencies.

  5. 5

    Consultancy collaborations

    Project-based collaborations to bring niche expertise into specific client engagements. Compensation can be project-based, daily rate, or success-based depending on scope. Most independent experts and specialized firms enter through this model.

What we expect from our partners

Long-term partner relationships are built on mutual standards. Five expectations that define every Provigood partnership.

Shared vision

Alignment with our commitment to excellence, integrity, and innovation. We don't work with partners who cut corners on quality or ethics, even when the project would be lucrative.

Complementary strengths

We add partners who bring skills, technologies, or networks that enhance our service offering, not duplicate what we already have. Specificity and depth matter more than breadth.

Demonstrated reliability

A proven track record of delivering quality and upholding trust. We will ask for client references, case studies, and examples of work. We will check them. This is not a formality.

Commitment to mutual growth

Dedication to achieving shared objectives and building long-term success, not extracting one-off margin. Our best partner relationships are 10+ years old.

Cultural sensitivity

Understanding and respect for the cultural dynamics of the Vietnamese market and ASEAN region. Foreign partners must demonstrate adaptive capacity; Vietnamese partners must be comfortable bridging across international cultures.

How we build a partnership

  1. 1

    Initial consultation

    A first 30-minute discovery call to discuss mutual goals, expectations, and potential collaboration areas. We share our project pipeline; you share your expertise and ambitions. No commitment, no pressure.

  2. 2

    Customized strategy development

    If alignment is clear, we co-create a working framework: partnership model, geographic scope, target client segments, expected workflow. Document it. Sign it. The partnership starts on a clear foundation.

  3. 3

    Resource allocation

    We define roles, responsibilities, and timelines for the first concrete collaboration. Specific clients, specific projects, specific deliverables. Provigood handles client relationship management; partners handle their domain expertise.

  4. 4

    Continuous communication

    Regular check-ins, feedback loops, project updates, and joint problem-solving. Partner relationships thrive on rhythm: quarterly reviews are the minimum, monthly is standard for active collaborations.

  5. 5

    Performance evaluation

    We measure outcomes against agreed objectives, identify improvements, and adapt the relationship as it grows. Successful partners get more projects, deeper integration, and Partner Spotlight visibility.

Frequently asked questions, Partnership

How do I become a Provigood partner? +

Apply via the form below or send us an email at contact@provigood.com with a brief description of your expertise, your geographic scope, and the type of partnership that interests you. We will set up a 30-minute discovery call within one to two weeks. If alignment is clear, we move to formal partnership documentation within four to six weeks.

What documents do I need to submit? +

For the initial application, a one-page company profile or personal CV, two or three project references (anonymized if needed), and a short paragraph on what you would bring to the Provigood network. For full partnership formalization, we also request audited financials (if applicable), proof of legal registration, and references we can call directly.

Are partnerships exclusive? +

Not by default. Most Provigood partnerships are non-exclusive: partners are free to work with other consultancies and firms. Exclusivity is negotiated only for specific contexts: distribution partnerships in defined geographies, joint venture investments, or category-defining client engagements. When exclusivity is on the table, it is matched with volume commitments and protected pricing.

How quickly will I receive my first project? +

It depends on the alignment with our pipeline. Some partners receive a first project within weeks of formalization; others wait two or three months for the right brief. We do not artificially route projects to underutilized partners: we route every brief to the partner best positioned to win it. The best partners build long, deep relationships from a foundation of mutual respect, not transactional volume.
